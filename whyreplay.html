Why REPLAY?

In a time where there is a rise in the demand of vinyl records, the trend is to buy and sell secondhand. But ever wondered why?

Buy secondhand

Buying secondhand vinyl records has a lot of advantages to it. Apart from them often being more affordable, you can also get unique finds that are rare or out-of-print. This way you may find a nostalgic record that you had forgotten about but now can happily replay. In another case you may discover something completely new that ends up being an all time favorite. Either way you never know what to expect as you explore the tons of secondhand vinyl records being sold at places like Concerto.
