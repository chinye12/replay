How to sell?

Concerto is the perfect place to resell your LP’s, if you’re looking to part with them. All you have to do is make a phone call so they can discuss the details with you. Depending on the size of your collection, an appointment will be set at the store or at your location.

Make sure to note that it’s very important to have an appointment scheduled and to bring your identification, as otherwise the sale can't be made. The payment can be through cash or a voucher, depending on your preference. In case you’ve any more questions I’d advise you to contact Concerto directly.
