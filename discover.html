A record to REPLAY

Want to replay but not sure where to start?
No worries, every season I’ll recommend you an amazing vinyl record to replay.

For the season of fall I bring to you ‘Everything You’ve Come To Expect’ by the Last Shadow Puppets. After hearing Miles Kane’s vocals on ’Dealer’ of 'Blue Banisters', an album by my favorite singer Lana del Rey, I went ahead and checked out more of his music. This brought me to the Last Shadow Puppets, a four member band including the famous Alex Turner of the Arctic Monkeys that we all know of. As well as James Ford and Zach Dawes who helped with the writing of 'Dealer'.

My favorite track of the record would be 'Miracle Aligner'. Fun fact! It’s also the first song that I heard of them. I find the meaning of the catchy lyrics mysterious till this day but it goes well combined with the beat that makes you want to sway along.

Tap the play button to listen to a preview of 'Miracle Aligner' by the Last Shadow Puppets.

As a runner up ‘The Dream Synopsis’ would be my pick of choice considering the lyrics. I interpret it as story where a man reminisces of his past secret lover. It seems that he can’t move on and wants to go back to that time based on the following lyrics:

‘Shoot through my mind and I can’t let go
Inseparable opposing images
When can you come back again?’

‘Everything You’ve Come To Expect’ is a diverse record as it includes baroque pop, psychedelic pop and orchestral soul. The studio album released back in 2016 including twelve tracks. The LP is currently available at Concerto for a price of 80 euro.
